What is Lofi Music?

Lofi (low fidelity) music is a music genre characterized by intentionally low-quality sound, including noise, distortion and vintage effects. Lofi hip hop has become a popular genre for background music during study, work or relaxation due to its relaxing and unobtrusive nature.
